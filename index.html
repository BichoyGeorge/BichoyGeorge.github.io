Global Demographic & Population Density Analysis

Excel Power Query Power BI
Business Objective

Mapped demographic distributions, annual net shifts, and spatial density dynamics across international borders using macro data from 2020.

Key Insights & Analytics
  • Scale of Analysis: Evaluated geospatial indicators across 235 distinct countries and regional territories.
  • Demographic Giants: Identified 14 countries whose populations surpass the 100M threshold. China held the global lead at 1.4B+ residents, representing 18.5% of the total global population footprint.
  • Density & Growth Anomalies: Bangladesh recorded the highest population density acceleration, tracking at 1,265 individuals per square mile. India demonstrated the highest net nominal annual growth, adding 14M residents.
  • Urbanization Split: Global urbanization metrics showed a clear division: 24.36% of the population resides in developed urban centers, compared to 75.64% living within rural and non-urban areas.
